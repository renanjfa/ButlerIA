ButlerIA

The project is based on a public dataset from Kaggle. The dataset idea is to analize the difference between
the two different text speeches in AI and Human. The idea of this project is to analize and build a tool to differentiate 
AI and Human text. 

First step is to download the dataset on Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hasanyiitakbulut/ai-and-human-text-dataset
